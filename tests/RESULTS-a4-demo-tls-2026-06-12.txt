  test (successful_handshake_captures_cert_not_after) PASSES on macOS per the A0.5

  Linux-only (GNU date -d dependency). The panel JSON, PromQL expression, and
  transformation are correct; the metric will appear when B1 atomic is emitted.
  This is an out-of-scope STOP item (see STOP items below).
